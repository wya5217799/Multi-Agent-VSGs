## ADR contents (summary of `2026-04-29-kundur-workspace-var-schema-boundary.md`)

Records 4 invariants:

1. **Scope**: schema covers env-side `apply_workspace_var` writes only;
   does NOT cover `bridge.warmup`, `BridgeConfig` template fields,
   NE39/SPS legacy variables, or MATLAB-side consumer registration.
2. **Two-tier validation**: name-valid (default) vs physically-effective
   (`require_effective=True`). The two are deliberately separate to
   handle v3 LoadStep dead channel.
3. **Hard rule**: `effective_in_profile` set advanced ONLY by physics
   fix; code-level refactors (C1/C4/C2 future work) must NOT modify
   this field.
4. **Forward implications**: C1 protocols will consume schema keys; C2
   discriminated union will use `profiles` field as source of truth;
   future bridge.warmup migration would need `STRUCT` family extension.
